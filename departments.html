Central Administration

Objective

General Administration, Planning, Budgeting & Coordinating, Legislative oversight.

Description

The General Administration sub-programme seeks to provide administrative support and ensure effective coordination of the activities of the various departments and quasi institutions under the District Assembly.

Key Functions

  • General Administration — overall coordination of Assembly activities
  • Planning & Budgeting — strategic planning and budget coordination
  • Legislative Oversight — Assembly meeting coordination
  • Procurement — public procurement management

Funding Sources

GoG Transfers, Assembly's Internally Generated Fund (IGF)

Physical Planning Department

Objective

To provide for the planning and sustainable development of land and human settlements.

Description

The Physical Planning Department exists to plan, manage and implement harmonious, sustainable and cost-effective human settlement and environmental horticulture in the district.

Key Functions

  • Human Settlement Planning
  • Land Use Regulation
  • Environmental Horticulture
  • Development Advisory

Staff & Funding

Staff Strength: 1 officer | Funding: GoG Transfers, DACF, Assembly's IGF

Finance Department

Objective

To provide financial management and accounting service to the Assembly and stakeholders.

Description

The Finance sub-programme provides financial services and accounting activities through the District Finance Office and Revenue Unit, responsible for budgeting, accounting, revenue mobilization and financial reporting.

Key Units

  • District Finance Office
  • Revenue Unit
  • Budget Unit
  • Accounts Unit

Staff & Funding

Staff Strength: 35 officers | Funding: GoG Transfers, Assembly's IGF

Human Resources Department

Objective

Ensure availability of competent workforce and meet staff needs and aspirations.

Description

The Human Resource Management sub-Programme covers recruitment, hiring, deploying, separation, development, compensation, motivation and managing employees of the District Assembly.

Key Units

  • Recruitment & Hiring
  • Staff Development
  • Performance Management
  • Staff Welfare

Staff & Funding

Staff Strength: 2 officers | Funding: GoG Transfers (DACF-RFG, GOG), Assembly's IGF

Health Department

Objective

To develop and maintain a clean, healthy, safe and pleasant physical environment for all residents.

Description

The Environmental Health & Sanitation Management unit promotes environmental health through sanitation management, disease control, health education and maintaining a clean, healthy environment.

Key Units

  • Environmental Sanitation
  • Disease Control
  • Health Education
  • Waste Management

Staff & Funding

Staff Strength: 17 officers | Funding: GoG allocations and IGF

Works Department

Objective

Facilitate sustainable and resilient infrastructure development.

Description

The Works Department facilitates sustainable and resilient infrastructure development and improves road transport infrastructure through contract management, project execution, supervision and regulation.

Key Units

  • Roads & Infrastructure
  • Buildings & Facilities
  • Contract Management
  • Project Supervision

Staff & Funding

Social Welfare & Community Development

Objective

To provide social protection and promote the social welfare of vulnerable groups and communities.

Description

The department delivers social protection programmes including safety net programmes, community development initiatives, child protection services and support for persons with disabilities across the district.

Key Units

  • Child Protection
  • Disability Support
  • Community Development
  • Safety Net Programmes

Births & Deaths Department

Objective

Effective vital registration of births and deaths in the district.

Description

The department is responsible for registration of all births and deaths occurring in the district and issuance of birth and death certificates to citizens.

Key Functions

  • Birth Registration
  • Death Registration
  • Certificate Issuance
  • Public Education on Vital Registration

Staff & Funding

Staff Strength: 2 officers | Funding: GoG allocations and IGF

Statistics Department

Objective

To collect, compile, analyze and disseminate accurate statistical data for planning and decision-making.

Description

The Statistics Department collects and manages demographic, health, economic and departmental data to support evidence-based planning and policy decisions in the district.

Key Data Areas

  • Demographic Data
  • Health Statistics
  • Economic Indicators
  • Departmental Data
