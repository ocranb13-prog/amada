Project Details

Report on Inauguration of Akroso Market Management Committee

26th November 2025 Akim Akroso Market Central Administration ✔ Completed

Background

On Wednesday, 26th November 2025, the Management of the Asene Manso Akroso District Assembly undertook an official visit to Akim Akroso to inaugurate the newly constituted Akroso Market Management Committee. The event was led by the District Coordinating Director (DCD), Ms. Gladys Asamoah Addai, in the absence of the District Chief Executive (DCE), Hon. Eric Ahinakwa, who was unavoidably absent due to other official engagements.

Purpose & Mandate

The inauguration marks a significant milestone in improving trade and commerce within the district. The newly constituted committee has been mandated to be responsible for the day-to-day management, maintenance, and development of the Akroso Market — ensuring it operates efficiently, safely and to the benefit of traders and consumers alike.

In Attendance

The ceremony was attended by local traders and market women, community leaders and elders, Assembly members for the area, department heads from the District Assembly, and representatives from the Ghana Revenue Authority and Municipal/District Trade departments.

Key Outcomes

  • Official swearing-in of the Akroso Market Management Committee members
  • Presentation of roles and responsibilities to committee members
  • Address by the DCD on the Assembly's commitment to market infrastructure development
  • Sensitization of traders on market rules, revenue obligations and sanitation standards
  • Call for community support in maintaining a clean, orderly and productive market environment

The District Assembly remains committed to providing the infrastructure and governance framework needed for our markets to thrive. This committee is a key part of that commitment.

— Ms. Gladys Asamoah Addai, District Coordinating Director
